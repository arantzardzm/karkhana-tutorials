Welcome to the Tutorial

Here, we will tell you about the most important materials in your kit!

Make sure to read all the descriptions carefully and hover over the characters to see what they have to say!

This is an arduino:

It acts as the brain of the entire system. You can program its behavior with code. It can then tell the rest of the components what to do.

This is a breadboard:

It is the connecting material between your arduino and your components. It is strong and able to conduct electric current.

This is a resistor:

It is the protector of the circuit. It can limit the amount of electricity going from one component to the other.

This are colorful leds:

They bring light to life. They can light up or down the way we want!

This is a temperature sensor:

It can detect the coldest and the hightest temperatures in the world.

Congratulations! Now you know what your kit components do!
