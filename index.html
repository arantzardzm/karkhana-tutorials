Tutorial

We are about to tell you about the most important materials in your kit!

Make sure to read the descriptions and click on the characters to see what they have to say!


This is an arduino:

It acts as the brain of the entire system. With code you can program its behavior. Then it can tell the rest of the components what to do!

This is a breadboard:

It is the connecting material between your arduino and your components. It is strong and able to conduct electric current!

This is a resistor:

This is what limits the amount of electricity going from one component to the other. It protects your circuit!

This are colorful leds:

They bring light to life! And there are many many colors.

This is a temperature sensor:

It can detect the coldest and the hightest temperatures in the world!

Congratulations! Now you know what your kit components do!
